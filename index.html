Memory Card Game

Think fast, flip smart, and find every matching pair! Can you beat the game before your friends? 😄

How to play!!

  • Rule 1:🎴 Flip two cards at a time.
  • Rule 2:✅ If they match, they stay open.
  • Rule 3:❌ If they don't match, they flip back.
  • Rule 4:🏆 Match all pairs to win the game.
  • Rule 5:⏱️ Finish with fewer moves and less time for a higher score.
